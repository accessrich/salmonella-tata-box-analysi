🔬 BIOLOGICAL INTERPRETATION
================================================================================

TATA BOX SIGNIFICANCE:
  • Ancient promoter element (found in eukaryotes and bacteria)
  • Consensus sequence: TATAAT (variable in real sequences)
  • Typically located ~10 bp upstream of transcription start site
  • Essential for transcription initiation in many genes

SALMONELLA FINDINGS:
  • TATA boxes are ABUNDANT (~22K matches per 4.8 Mb genome)
  • Distribution is UNIFORM (no strand bias, normal distribution)
  • Organization is CONSERVED (consistent spacing ~219 bp median)
  • Strains are SIMILAR (92% in 20.5K-23K range)

IMPLICATIONS:
  1. Highly stable promoter architecture across Salmonella strains
  2. Consistent gene regulation machinery across pathogenic variants
  3. Target for evolutionary studies (promoter element conservation)
  4. Useful for comparative genomics and phylogenetic analysis
  5. Foundation for understanding virulence factor regulation
