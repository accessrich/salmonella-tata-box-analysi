INTERPRETATION GUIDE
================================================================================

✓ NORMAL DISTRIBUTION
  The Q-Q plot shows points aligned with the diagonal, confirming
  that TATA counts follow a normal distribution. This suggests
  evolutionary stability of promoter elements.

✓ STRAND BALANCE
  Perfect 50:50 distribution on both strands indicates no selective
  pressure favoring one strand over the other. Typical for bacterial
  genomes with roughly equal numbers of genes on each strand.

✓ CONSERVED SPACING
  Median spacing of 219 bp between TATA boxes is consistent with
  typical bacterial gene spacing (200-500 bp). Indicates maintained
  regulatory organization across Salmonella strains.

✓ ABUNDANCE CONSERVATION
  Small differences between high/medium/low groups (20-23K range)
  suggest that TATA box content is highly conserved across
  Salmonella enterica strains despite genomic variations.
